FAQs
  1. How do you file your VAT Return?
    One experienced accountant from Marzan Consulting will be handling your accounts. We will file and submit your VAT return to HMRC directly.
  2. Vat registration, what is it?
    If your annual turnover is above the threshold set by the government, then your business should get registered for VAT.
  3. What do we mean by submitting your VAT return online?
    Marzan consulting will work out and submit your VAT returns to HMRC. We shall also inform you when the liability is due to be paid to HMRC.

Overview

Payroll is the one aspect of any firm that cannot be late or incorrect under any circumstances. For many of our UK clients, we manage and execute payroll. In layman's words, we can handle everything for you, even dealing with HMRC on your behalf.

Why Payroll Outsourcing is Important?

Firms should be more productive and responsive if they want to stay on top of the market. Marzan helps you save time, money, and people so you can focus on client-satisfactory work.

Marzan payroll gives your accounting department access to a data dashboard that provides insights into how to prepare for the future, enhance employees, and make accurate salary payments.

You won't have to worry about finding in-house payroll expertise if you switch to Marzan. Also, your employees won't have to worry about the mundane aspects of payroll, such as ever changing tax rules, trends, and industry problems.

What Do We Offer?

Payroll is more than just paying your employees, and you didn't get into the business to deal with paperwork and comply with payroll outsourcing services laws. We provide:


  1. Furlough Claims
  2. Payroll Enrollment
  3. Pay slips Generation
  4. Submitting monthly/weekly/RTI returns to HRMC
  5. Year-end submissions to the HRMC
  6. Enrollment of pension
  7. Submission of weekly/monthly pension to the government
  8. PAYE registration/ deregistration
  9. CIS registration/deregistration

FAQs

  1. Why outsource payroll processing?
    Remove the liability of making weekly/monthly deposits and the stress related to it and trust an outsourcing professional who will work on these functions 24*7. You can moreover save time and focus on important business functions such as operations and channelizing the way to acquire the customers
  2. How does payroll outsourcing work?
    We will take care of these functions from A-Z, we will input the data on a secured software, generate and enroll pay slips which will be generated using a software that is HMRC compliant. We will submit the same to the government as well as take care of the pension plan and your year-end hassles too. We are certified on SAGE.
  3. Can you track even the holiday and sick pay?
    Yes, of course, our software is very flexible. You tell us in proper documentation these details and we will take care of these when generating the pay slips.
  4. Are our services local or not limited to a particular space?
    At Marzan Consulting we have clients who are spread across the globe, and they function in different verticals. So, be rest ensured we will take care of everything even the changing rules and other dynamics of that country.
